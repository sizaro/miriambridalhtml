About Us

At Miriam Bridal Services, we specialize in creating unforgettable moments for brides on their most special day. Our mission is to deliver personalized, top-quality bridal services tailored to meet the unique needs of every bride.

Our Story

Miriam Bridal Services was founded in 2020 by Miriam Aliba, with the goal of offering exceptional bridal hair, makeup, and fashion consulting services. Over the years, we have helped countless brides make their dream weddings a reality.

We believe in a personal touch, and our dedicated team works closely with brides to create the perfect look for their big day.

Our Mission

To make every bride feel like the best version of herself on her wedding day by offering top-tier services that are both luxurious and affordable.

Our Vision

To be a leading bridal service provider recognized for exceptional quality, innovation, and care, with a growing community of happy brides.
